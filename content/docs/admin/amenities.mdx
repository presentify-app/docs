title: Amenities Manager
description: Create custom equipment tags for desks and parking spots beyond the built-in options.
---

The Amenities Manager lets you define workspace-specific equipment and amenity tags. These custom tags extend the built-in equipment options and appear as filters in the desk and parking booking views.

> Custom amenities require a **Pro** plan.

## Built-in vs. custom amenities

**Built-in desk amenities** (always available):
Monitor, Docking Station, Dual Screen, Phone, Standing Desk, Ergonomic, Accessible, Quiet Zone, Window Seat

**Built-in parking amenities** (always available):
Accessible, EV Charging

**Custom amenities** are additional tags you create for your workspace — for example, "Monitor Arm", "Whiteboard", "Privacy Screen", or "Covered Parking".

## Creating a custom amenity

1. Go to **Settings** > **Resources/Equipment**
2. Click **Add Amenity**
3. Configure:
   - **Name** (required) — The display name (max 255 characters)
   - **Icon** — Choose from: Tag, Wifi, Coffee, Printer, Headphones, Lightbulb, Plug, Lock, Fan, Flower
   - **Resource Type** — Desk, Parking, or both
4. Click **Save**

## Managing amenities

For each custom amenity you can:

- **Edit** — Change the name, icon, or resource type
- **Deactivate** — Toggle off without deleting. Deactivated amenities are hidden from booking filters but preserved on existing resources.
- **Delete** — Remove permanently

## Assigning amenities to resources

After creating custom amenities, assign them to desks or parking spots:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Locations** and navigate to a desk or parking spot
2. In the resource editor, toggle custom amenities on or off
3. Save

Custom amenities are stored alongside built-in equipment and appear in the booking view filters.

## How members use custom amenities

Custom amenities appear in the desk and parking booking filter panels alongside built-in equipment. Members can filter by custom amenities to find desks or spots with specific features.

Members can also set custom amenities as preferences in their [profile](/docs/employee/profile), so they're pre-selected when opening the booking view.
