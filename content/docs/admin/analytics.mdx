The admin analytics area gives Admins and Owners a high-level view of workspace performance. Access it via **Admin** > **Analytics** in the sidebar. The analytics navigation includes tabs for **Overview**, **Funnel**, **Attribution**, **Revenue**, **Workspaces**, **Cohorts**, and **Users**.

> Admin analytics requires the **Admin** or **Owner** role.

## Revenue

The **Revenue** tab (`/admin/analytics/revenue`) provides a real-time snapshot of subscription and trial metrics.

### KPI cards

Four headline metrics are displayed at the top:

| KPI | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| **MRR** | Monthly Recurring Revenue across all active subscriptions. Yearly subscriptions are normalized to a monthly amount. Shows the total number of active subscriptions below. |
| **ARPU** | Average Revenue Per User — MRR divided by the number of active subscriptions. Shows total seats below. |
| **Trial Conversion** | Percentage of expired trials that converted to a paid subscription. Shows the converted count out of total expired trials. |
| **Churned** | Number of cancelled subscriptions. |

### Trial Funnel

Below the KPI cards, the trial funnel visualizes the progression of trial workspaces:

- **Trials Started** — Total workspaces that began a trial
- **Trials Expired** — Trials that have passed their end date
- **Trials Converted** — Expired trials that converted to an active subscription
