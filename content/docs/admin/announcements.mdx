---
title: Announcements
description: Post location-specific announcements that appear as banners for members.
---

Announcements let you communicate important information to members at specific locations. They appear as banner notifications on the Dashboard and relevant booking pages.

## Creating an announcement

1. Go to **Settings** > **Locations**
2. Select a location
3. Navigate to the **Announcements** section
4. Click **Add Announcement**
5. Configure:

| Field | Required | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **Title** | Yes | Short headline (max 255 characters) |
| **Body** | No | Detailed message (max 2,000 characters) |
| **Type** | Yes | Info (blue), Warning (yellow), or Critical (red) |
| **Start Date** | No | When the announcement becomes visible |
| **End Date** | No | When the announcement expires |

6. Click **Save**

If no start/end dates are set, the announcement is permanent until manually deactivated or deleted.

## Announcement types

| Type | Color | Use case |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Info** | Blue | General information (e.g., "New coffee machine on Floor 2") |
| **Warning** | Yellow | Important notices (e.g., "Elevator maintenance on Thursday") |
| **Critical** | Red | Urgent alerts (e.g., "Office closed due to weather") |

## Managing announcements

For each announcement you can:

- **Edit** — Update the title, body, type, or dates
- **Deactivate** — Toggle off without deleting. Deactivated announcements are not shown to members.
- **Delete** — Remove permanently

## Where announcements appear

Active announcements are displayed as banners at the top of the **Dashboard** for members who are associated with the announcement's location. Multiple announcements can be stacked.

## Time-based visibility

An announcement is shown when all conditions are met:

1. The announcement is **active** (not deactivated)
2. The **start date** has passed (or is not set)
3. The **end date** has not passed (or is not set)

Use scheduled announcements for planned events — set the start and end dates in advance and the announcement appears and disappears automatically.
