The Billing tab shows your current plan, subscription status, and usage. Access it via **Settings** > **Billing**.

## Current plan

The top card shows:

- **Plan name** — Free Starter, Pro, or Enterprise
- **Status** — Trial, Active, Past Due, Canceled, or Managed (Enterprise)
- **Active members** — Current count vs. plan limit
- **MAU** (Monthly Active Users) — Billed user count for Admins

## Plan comparison

Three plan cards summarise each plan. Expand the **Feature comparison** section to see a detailed side-by-side table of all included features.

### Free Starter (€0/month)
- Up to 25 members, 1 location
- Presence tracking, Who's In, Desk pools
- Team calendar, Notifications

### Pro (per active user/month)
- Unlimited members and locations
- Individual desks, Parking, Floor plans
- Check-in, Analytics, Booking policies, Team days
- Monthly or yearly billing (yearly with discount)

### Enterprise (custom pricing)
- Everything in Pro
- API access, Webhooks, SCIM
- Custom branding, Dedicated support, SLA

## Upgrading to Pro

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Upgrade** on the Pro plan card
3. Choose monthly or yearly billing
4. Complete the Paddle checkout (payment details, address, tax)
5. Your workspace is upgraded immediately

Presentify uses [Paddle](https://www.paddle.com) for payment processing. Paddle handles invoicing, tax calculation, and receipts.

## Switching billing interval

If you're on Pro, you can switch between monthly and yearly billing:

1. In the Billing tab, toggle the **Monthly / Yearly** switch
2. The change takes effect at your next billing date
3. Yearly billing includes a discount

## Downgrading to Free Starter

Before downgrading, your workspace must meet Free Starter limits:

- Maximum **25 members**
- Maximum **1 location**
- Maximum **3 floors**
- Maximum **25 desks**

If your workspace exceeds these limits, you'll need to reduce resources first. To downgrade:

1. Click **Downgrade** on the Free Starter card
2. Review the warnings (feature loss, member limits)
3. Confirm the downgrade

Pro features become unavailable immediately, but no data is deleted.

## Monthly Active Users (MAU)

Presentify bills based on MAU — the number of members who actively used the platform in a calendar month. A member counts as active if they:

- Created a non-cancelled booking, **or**
- Set a presence entry

Inactive members are not billed. The MAU count is synced to Paddle automatically.

## FAQ

Below the payment history, a **FAQ** section answers common questions about trials, billing cycles, per-user pricing, cancellations, downgrades, and invoices.

The FAQ is not shown for workspaces on the Enterprise plan.

## Enterprise

Enterprise plans are managed manually. Contact [support@presentify.org](mailto:support@presentify.org) for custom pricing, API access, webhooks, and SLA agreements.
