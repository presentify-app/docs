---
title: Desk Assignments
description: Permanently or temporarily assign individual desks to specific members.
---

Desk assignments let you reserve a specific desk for a member. Only **individual** desk types can be assigned. This is useful for employees who need a dedicated workspace.

> Desk assignments require a **Pro** plan and the **policies** feature.

## Creating an assignment

1. Go to **Settings** > **Policies** > **Desk Assignments** (or the desk assignment manager)
2. Select a location and floor
3. Individual desks on the floor are listed with their current assignment status
4. Click **Assign Desk** on an unassigned desk
5. Select a member from the searchable list
6. Configure:
   - **Permanent** — Toggle on for a permanent assignment (no end date)
   - **Release on OOO** — Toggle on to auto-release the desk when the member marks themselves as Away
7. Click **Save**

## Permanent vs. temporary assignments

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Permanent** | The desk is assigned indefinitely until manually removed |
| **Temporary** | The desk is assigned for a specific date range (valid from / valid until) |

Only one member can be assigned to a desk at any given time. Overlapping assignments on the same desk are prevented.

## OOO release

When **Release on OOO** is enabled on an assignment:

- If the assigned member sets their presence status to **Away**, the desk is automatically released to the pool
- Other members can book the desk during the absence
- When the assigned member returns, their assignment is still active — the desk remains theirs

This also requires the location-level policy **Assigned Desk OOO Release** to be enabled (default: on).

## Removing an assignment

Click the delete button next to an assigned desk to remove the assignment. The desk becomes available for pool booking again.

## Who can be assigned?

Any active workspace member can be assigned to an individual desk. The desk must be of type **Individual** — Pool and Hot Desk types cannot be assigned.
