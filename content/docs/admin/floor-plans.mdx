Floor plans will provide a visual booking experience where members can see the office layout and click on desks to book them.

> Floor plans are **coming soon** and not yet available. This page describes the planned functionality.

## Planned features

- **Upload floor plan images** — Upload PNG, JPG, or SVG images of your office layouts
- **Place desk markers** — Drag and position desks on the floor plan with x/y coordinates and rotation
- **Visual booking** — Members see desks on the map and click to book
- **Zone highlighting** — Zones color-coded by type (Quiet Zone, Collaboration, Focus, etc.)
- **Colleague pins** — See where colleagues are booked with avatar pins on the map

## Current status

The floor plan editor is in development. In the meantime, members can book desks using the **list view**, which provides full filtering by location, floor, equipment, zone, and availability.

Floor plans will require a **Pro** plan.
