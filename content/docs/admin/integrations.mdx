title: Integrations

The **Integrations** tab in workspace settings lets admins connect external services to Presentify. It is available to workspace Admins and Owners on the **Pro** or **Enterprise** plan.

Navigate to **Settings** > **Integrations** to manage connections.

## Microsoft 365

The Microsoft 365 section groups all integrations that connect to your organization's Microsoft tenant.

### Microsoft Teams Presence Sync

Automatically set your team members' Microsoft Teams availability status based on their planned presence in Presentify. See [Microsoft Teams — Presence Sync](/docs/integrations/teams#presence-sync) for the full feature overview.

#### Connecting a tenant

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Integrations**
2. Under **Microsoft 365**, find your organization's tenant in the **Microsoft Tenants in this Workspace** list
3. Click **Connect with Microsoft** — this opens the Microsoft admin consent screen
4. A Microsoft 365 admin must approve the requested permissions (`Presence.ReadWrite.All`)
5. After consent, the tenant shows as **Connected**

The admin who clicks **Connect with Microsoft** must be both a Presentify workspace Admin/Owner and authorized to grant app permissions in Microsoft 365 (typically a Global Administrator or Application Administrator).

Presentify detects tenants automatically based on which Microsoft work accounts your members use to sign in. Personal Microsoft accounts (outlook.com, hotmail.com, live.com) are filtered out.

#### Configuring the sync

Once connected, a connection card appears with these settings:

- **Sync enabled** — Toggle the sync on or off for this tenant
- **Set status at** — Time of day when presence is pushed to Teams (default 07:00, evaluated in each member's local timezone)
- **Clear status at** — Time when Teams status is reset (default 18:00)
- **Status Mapping** — Expand to customize which Teams availability (Available, Busy, Do Not Disturb, Be Right Back, Away, Offline) maps to each Presentify status

Click **Save** after making changes.

#### Verifying consent

Click **Verify consent** on a connection card to check that the admin consent is still valid. If consent has expired or been revoked, the admin will need to re-authorize.

A daily health check also runs automatically and flags any connections with consent issues.

#### Member opt-in stats

The connection card shows **Members opted in** — how many active members have enabled presence sync out of the total.

#### Sync Log

The **Sync Log** section at the bottom of the Integrations tab shows recent Teams presence sync activity, including which member was synced, the action taken (set or clear), and whether it succeeded. Click **Show more** to load additional entries.

#### Disconnecting a tenant

1. Click **Disconnect** on the connection card
2. Confirm in the dialog

This removes the Teams presence sync for all members using that tenant. The action cannot be undone — to reconnect, the Microsoft 365 admin must grant consent again.
