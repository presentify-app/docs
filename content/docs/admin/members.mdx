title: Members

The Members tab is where you manage who has access to your workspace and what they can do. Access it via **Settings** > **Members**.

## Member list

All active and invited members are shown in a table with:

- **Name** (or email if name not set)
- **Email**
- **Role** badge (Owner, Admin, Office Admin, Manager, Member)
- **Status** badge (Active or Invited)
- **Actions** menu

## Inviting members

### By email

1. Click **Invite Member**
2. Enter the email address
3. Click **Invite**

The person receives a sign-in link. If they don't have a Presentify account yet, one is created automatically. The member starts with the **Member** role and "Invited" status until they sign in for the first time.

### By invite link

In the [Setup Wizard](/docs/setup/invite) or during onboarding, you can generate a shareable invite link. Anyone with the link can join your workspace.

### Plan limits

The Free Starter plan allows up to **25 members**. Pro and Enterprise have no member limit. If you've reached your plan's limit, you'll need to [upgrade](/docs/admin/billing) before inviting more members.

## Changing roles

1. Click the actions menu next to a member
2. Select the new role

Role restrictions:
- Only the **Owner** can assign or remove the Owner role
- **Admins** can assign any role except Owner
- You cannot change your own role
- The last Owner cannot be demoted

When changing a member to **Office Admin**, you'll be prompted to assign locations. See [Roles & Permissions](/docs/roles) for what each role can do.

## Assigning Office Admin locations

When a member has the Office Admin role:

1. Click the actions menu > **Manage Locations**
2. Check the locations this Office Admin should manage
3. Click **Save**

The Office Admin can only view and cancel bookings at their assigned locations.

## Assigning a manager

1. Click the actions menu > **Assign Manager**
2. Search for and select a manager from the member list
3. Click **Save**

The manager relationship is used for:
- Delegation: Managers can book desks/parking on behalf of their direct reports
- Team visibility in the team calendar

Circular manager chains are prevented — you cannot set A as B's manager if B is already A's manager (directly or indirectly).

## Editing member settings

Admins can edit a member's personal settings:

1. Click the actions menu > **Edit Settings**
2. Modify any of:
   - Language and timezone
   - Date and time format
   - Team assignment
   - Default location
   - Weekly digest preference
   - Privacy settings (Show in Who's In, Show Desk on Map, etc.)
3. Click **Save**

## Member defaults

Configure default settings applied to newly invited members:

- Default language and timezone
- Default team
- Default location
- Weekly digest enabled/disabled

These defaults are applied when a new member is invited. Existing members are not affected.

## Removing members

1. Click the actions menu > **Remove**
2. Confirm in the dialog

Removed members are set to "inactive" (soft delete). Their historical data (bookings, presence entries) is preserved. The member can be re-invited later.

You cannot remove yourself or the last Owner.
