---
title: Office Policy
description: Set a minimum number of office days per week for your workspace.
---

The Office Policy defines how many days per week members should be in the office. This is a workspace-wide setting that helps enforce hybrid work guidelines.

## Setting the requirement

1. Go to **Settings** > **Policies**
2. In the **Office Policy** card, select the minimum number of office days:
   - **No Policy** — No requirement (default)
   - **1 day** through **5 days** — Minimum office days per week

The setting applies to all members across the workspace.

## How compliance is displayed

When an office policy is active, a compliance badge appears on the **My Week** card in the Presence Planner:

| Badge | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Green** | Member meets or exceeds the requirement |
| **Amber** | Member is one day short of the requirement |
| **Red** | Member is below the requirement |

The badge shows "X/Y office days required" to help members plan their week.

## What counts as an office day?

Only these presence statuses count toward the office day requirement:

- **In Office**
- **Onsite without Desk**

Remote, On the Road, Customer Site, Training/Event, and Away do **not** count.

## Enforcement

The office policy is informational — it shows compliance indicators but does not block members from setting non-office statuses. Members are encouraged to meet the target through the visual compliance badge, not through restrictions.
