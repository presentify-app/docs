title: QR Codes
description: Generate and print QR codes for desk and parking check-in.

QR codes enable members to check in at their booked desk or parking spot by scanning a physical code. Access the QR code manager via **Settings** > **QR Codes**.

> QR codes require a **Pro** plan with check-in enabled in your [booking policies](/docs/admin/policies).

## How it works

Each desk and parking spot gets a unique QR code that encodes a check-in URL:

- **Desk QR**: `https://presentify.org/api/checkin/desk/{deskId}`
- **Parking QR**: `https://presentify.org/api/checkin/parking/{spotId}`

When a member scans the code with their phone camera, Presentify finds their booking for today and checks them in automatically.

## Generating QR codes

1. Go to **Settings** > **QR Codes**
2. Switch between the **Desks** and **Parking** tabs
3. QR codes are generated for all resources automatically

QR codes are generated client-side for performance — no server load.

## Filtering

Use filters to find specific QR codes:

- **Search** — Type a desk or spot name to filter
- **Location** — Filter by office location
- **Type** — Filter by desk type (Pool, Individual, Hot Desk) or parking type (Car, Bike, etc.)

## Printing QR codes

### Print all

Click **Print All** to open the system print dialog with all filtered QR codes laid out for printing. Each QR code includes the resource name and location for easy identification.

### Download individual

Click the download button on a specific QR code to save it as a PNG image. Use this for custom printing or labeling.

## Placing QR codes

Print the QR codes and place them at the physical desk or parking spot. Members scan the code with their phone when they arrive to complete their check-in.

Best practices:
- Place desk QR codes on the desk surface or monitor stand
- Place parking QR codes on the parking barrier or pillar near the spot
- Use a protective sleeve or laminate for durability
- Ensure the QR code is scannable from a comfortable distance

## Who can access QR codes

- **Admin / Owner** — Can generate QR codes for all locations
- **Office Admin** — Can generate QR codes only for their assigned locations
