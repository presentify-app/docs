This page covers how admins manage roles. For a full description of what each role can do, see [Roles & Permissions](/docs/roles).

## Assigning roles

1. Go to **Settings** > **Members**
2. Click the actions menu next to a member
3. Select the desired role

Available roles: **Owner**, **Admin**, **Office Admin**, **Manager**, **Member**.

## Office Admin setup

When you assign the Office Admin role, you must also assign locations:

1. Change the member's role to **Office Admin**
2. Click **Manage Locations** in the actions menu
3. Select one or more locations
4. Click **Save**

Without assigned locations, the Office Admin cannot access any location-specific features. You can update assigned locations at any time.

## Manager setup

To enable delegation features, assign a manager to each team member:

1. Click **Assign Manager** in the member's actions menu
2. Select the manager from the searchable list
3. Click **Save**

This enables the manager to:
- Book desks and parking on behalf of their direct reports
- See their direct reports in the team calendar

To remove a manager assignment, select **No Manager** in the dialog.

## Ownership transfer

Only the current Owner can transfer ownership:

1. Change another member's role to **Owner**
2. The previous Owner is automatically demoted to **Admin**

There must always be exactly one Owner per workspace.

## Best practices

- Assign **Admin** to IT leads or HR managers who need full configuration access
- Use **Office Admin** for local office managers who only need to manage their specific locations
- Use **Manager** for team leads who need analytics access and delegation
- Keep the **Member** role for everyone else — it covers all day-to-day features
