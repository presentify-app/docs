---
title: Calendar Subscription
description: Sync your Presentify presence and bookings to Outlook, Google, or Apple Calendar.
---

Calendar subscription lets you view your Presentify schedule in your favorite calendar app. Your presence entries and desk/parking bookings are synced automatically via an iCal feed.

## Setting up a subscription

1. Go to your **Profile** page
2. Scroll to the **Calendar Subscription** section
3. Click **Generate Token** to create your calendar URL
4. Copy the `webcal://` URL

## Adding to your calendar

### Outlook

1. In Outlook, go to **Calendar** > **Add calendar** > **Subscribe from web**
2. Paste the `webcal://` URL
3. Give it a name (e.g., "Presentify")
4. Click **Import**

### Google Calendar

1. In Google Calendar, click **+** next to "Other calendars" > **From URL**
2. Paste the URL
3. Click **Add calendar**

### Apple Calendar

1. In Calendar, go to **File** > **New Calendar Subscription**
2. Paste the URL
3. Click **Subscribe**

## What's synced

The calendar feed includes:

- Your presence entries (In Office, Remote, Away, etc.)
- Your desk bookings with desk name and location
- Your parking bookings

Events are read-only — changes must be made in Presentify.

## Revoking the subscription

If you want to stop sharing your calendar or regenerate the URL:

1. Go to your **Profile** page
2. Click **Revoke** next to your calendar subscription
3. The old URL stops working immediately
4. Generate a new token if you want to re-subscribe

Revoking is useful if you shared the URL with someone who should no longer have access.
