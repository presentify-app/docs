---
title: Check-in
description: Confirm your arrival at the office via button click or QR code scan.
---

Check-in confirms that you've arrived at your booked desk or parking spot. Your admin configures whether check-in is required and which mode is used.

> Check-in requires a **Pro** plan.

## Check-in modes

| Mode | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **None** | No check-in required. Bookings are confirmed at booking time. |
| **Manual** | Click the Check-In button on your booking to confirm arrival. |
| **QR Required** | Scan the QR code at your desk or parking spot to check in. |

## Manual check-in

1. Open the **Dashboard** or go to **Desks** > **My Bookings**
2. Find your booking for today
3. Click the **Check In** button
4. Your booking status changes from "Confirmed" to "Checked In"

## QR code check-in

If your admin has placed QR codes on desks or parking spots:

1. Scan the QR code with your phone's camera
2. The Presentify check-in page opens
3. Your booking is automatically verified and checked in
4. You see a confirmation screen

The QR code links to your workspace's check-in page with the desk or parking spot pre-selected.

## Check-in window

Check-in is available starting from the configured window (default: 30 minutes before the booking date). If you don't check in within the window, your booking may be auto-released.

## Auto-release on no-show

If check-in is required and you don't check in:

1. Your booking stays in "Confirmed" status
2. After the no-show threshold (default: 3 days), the booking is marked as **No Show**
3. The desk or parking spot is released back to the pool for others to book

This ensures that unused bookings don't block resources for your colleagues.

## Booking status flow

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Confirmed** | Booking is active, not yet checked in |
| **Checked In** | You've confirmed your arrival |
| **Completed** | The booking date has passed |
| **Cancelled** | You or an admin cancelled the booking |
| **No Show** | Check-in required but not performed within the threshold |
| **Auto Released** | No-show booking released back to pool |
