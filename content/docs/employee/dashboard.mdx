The dashboard is your starting point in Presentify. It shows your status, today's bookings, who's in the office, and your team's weekly stats.

## My Status Today

Shows your current presence status for today. If you haven't set a status yet, you'll see three quick-action buttons:

- **In Office** — You're working from the office
- **Remote** — You're working from home or another location
- **Away** — You're out of office

Click any button to set your status instantly. For more options (half-days, specific locations, other statuses), use the [Presence Planner](/docs/employee/presence).

## This Week

A 5-day overview (Monday through Friday) showing your plans for the current week:

- **Status** with icon and label for each day
- **Location** name (if associated with your presence entry)
- **Desk booking** with desk name (if booked)
- **Parking booking** with spot name (if booked)
- **Team Day** badge (if the day is marked as a team day)

Today is highlighted with a colored ring. Team days are marked with a violet border.

## Quick Actions

Four buttons for fast access to the most common actions:

- **Set Presence** — Opens the presence planner
- **Book Desk** — Opens desk booking
- **Book Parking** — Opens parking booking
- **Who Is In** — Opens the Who's In view

## Today's Bookings

Only shown if you have active bookings for today. Lists your confirmed and checked-in desk and parking bookings with:

- Resource name and floor/location
- Status badge (Confirmed or Checked In)
- **Check-In** button if check-in is required and you haven't checked in yet

## Colleagues Today

Shows colleagues who have set a presence status for today. You can filter by:

- **All Colleagues** — Everyone in the workspace
- **My Team** — Only your team members
- **Favorites** — Only colleagues you've starred

Each colleague card shows their name, status, team, and location. If a colleague has booked a desk, you'll see a **Book Near** button to find a desk on the same floor.

Click the star icon to add or remove a colleague from your favorites. Favorites are sorted to the top.

## My Favorites

Shows your favorited desks and parking spots with their current availability. Available resources link directly to the booking page.

## Team Week Stats

A weekly breakdown showing how many team members are in the office each day (Monday through Friday):

- **Bar chart** with percentage fill per day
- **Team average** vs. **Workspace average** comparison
- **Trend indicator** showing whether attendance is up, down, or stable compared to last week

## Announcements

If your workspace admin has posted location announcements, they appear as banners at the top of the dashboard.
