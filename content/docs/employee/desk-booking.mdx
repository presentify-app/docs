---
title: Desk Booking
description: Find and reserve a desk at your office — with filters, floor plans, and guest booking.
---

Desk booking lets you reserve a workspace in advance. You can browse available desks, filter by equipment and zone, and book for yourself, a colleague, or a guest.

## Booking a desk

1. Navigate to **Desks** in the sidebar
2. Select a **location** from the dropdown (your default location is pre-selected)
3. Choose a **date** using the date navigation (today, previous/next day)
4. Browse available desks in the list
5. Click a desk to open the booking dialog
6. Confirm your booking

Your booking appears immediately in **My Bookings** and on your dashboard.

## Desk types

| Type | Description | Plan |
|------|-------------|------|
| **Pool** | Shared desk — anyone can book, first-come-first-served | Free + Pro |
| **Individual** | Assigned to a specific person. Only the assigned member can book it. | Pro |
| **Drop-in** | Flexible desk for immediate use | Pro |

## Filters

Use filters to narrow down the desk list:

- **Floor** — Show desks on a specific floor only
- **Desk Type** — Pool, Individual, or Drop-in
- **Availability** — Available or Booked
- **Equipment** — Filter by one or more:
  - Monitor, Docking Station, Dual Screen, Standing Desk, Ergonomic, Accessible, Quiet Zone, Window Seat
- **Zone Type** — Neighborhood, Quiet Zone, Collaboration, Focus, Social, Meeting
- **Neighborhood** — Filter by named desk neighborhoods
- **Team** — Show desks in zones reserved for a specific team
- **Favorites Only** — Toggle to show only desks you've starred

Equipment filters use AND logic — selecting "Monitor" and "Standing" shows only desks that have both.

## Booking dialog

When you click a desk, the booking dialog opens with:

- **Desk name**, type badge, floor, and zone
- **Privacy toggle** — Mark the booking as private so others can't see it (if enabled by your admin)
- **Book for** options:
  - **Myself** (default)
  - **Colleague** — Search and select a team member (Manager/Admin/Owner only)
  - **Guest** — Enter guest name, email (optional), and company (optional)
- **Add Parking** checkbox — If your location uses basic parking mode, you can auto-book a parking spot alongside your desk
- **Notes** — Optional booking notes (up to 500 characters)

## My Bookings

The **My Bookings** tab shows your upcoming desk and parking bookings grouped by date. For each booking you can:

- **Check In** — Confirm your arrival (if check-in is required)
- **Reschedule** — Switch to a different desk on the same date
- **Cancel** — Cancel the booking
- **Share** — Copy a link to share your booking with colleagues

## Guest booking

If guest booking is enabled in your workspace, Admins and Managers can book desks for external guests:

1. Click a desk and open the booking dialog
2. Select **Guest** under "Book for"
3. Enter the guest's name (required), email and company (optional)
4. Confirm the booking

Guest bookings count toward the daily guest booking limit set by your admin (default: 3 per day).

## Booking rules

Your admin may have configured booking policies that affect your experience:

- **Advance booking window** — How far in advance you can book (default: 14 days, priority members: 30 days)
- **Max bookings per day** — Maximum desk bookings allowed per day (default: 1)
- **Check-in required** — Whether you need to check in after booking (see [Check-in](/docs/employee/check-in))
- **Zone restrictions** — Some zones may be restricted to specific teams
