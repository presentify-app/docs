---
title: Favorites
description: Bookmark colleagues, desks, and parking spots for quick access.
---

Favorites let you bookmark the people and resources you use most often. Favorited items are highlighted and sorted to the top across Presentify.

## Favoriting colleagues

Click the star icon next to a colleague's name in:

- **Dashboard** > Colleagues Today
- **Who's In** page
- **Team** calendar

Favorited colleagues appear at the top of colleague lists and can be filtered with the "Favorites" filter.

## Favoriting desks and parking spots

Click the star icon on a desk or parking spot card in the booking view. Favorited resources:

- Appear in the **My Favorites** widget on your dashboard with current availability
- Can be filtered with the **Favorites Only** toggle in the booking view
- Are highlighted in the desk/parking list

## Managing favorites

Favorites are per-workspace — each workspace has its own set. To remove a favorite, click the star icon again. The star turns from filled (yellow) to outline.

There is no separate favorites management page — favorites are toggled directly on the items wherever they appear.
