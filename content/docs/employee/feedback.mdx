The Feedback section lets you submit feature ideas, vote on suggestions from others, and discuss improvements. Your input helps shape the product roadmap.

## Browsing ideas

1. Navigate to **Feedback** in the sidebar
2. Browse the list of submitted ideas
3. Use the **search bar** to find ideas by keyword
4. Sort by **Top** (most voted) or **Newest**
5. Click an idea to view its details, vote, or comment

## Submitting a new idea

1. Click the **New Idea** button
2. Enter a **Title** — a short, descriptive summary of your idea
3. Add a **Description** — explain your idea in detail, what problem it solves, and who benefits
4. Optionally select up to 3 **Topics** to categorize your idea
5. Click **Submit Idea**

### Duplicate check

As you type your title and description, Presentify automatically searches for similar existing ideas. If potential duplicates are found, a warning panel appears listing the matching ideas with their current status.

Review the listed ideas carefully. If your idea is genuinely different, check the confirmation box ("I have checked the existing ideas and confirm this is not a duplicate.") to enable the **Submit Idea** button. If your idea already exists, consider voting or commenting on the existing entry instead of submitting a new one.

## Voting

Click the vote button on any idea to upvote it. Click again to remove your vote. The vote count shows how many people support the idea.

## Commenting

Open an idea's detail view to leave a comment. Comments let you add context, ask questions, or express support for a specific aspect of the idea.

## Idea statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Submitted** | The idea has been submitted and is awaiting review |
| **Under Review** | The team is actively evaluating the idea |
| **Planned** | The idea has been accepted and is on the roadmap |
| **Shipped** | The idea has been implemented and released |
