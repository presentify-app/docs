---
title: Floor Plan View
description: Book desks using an interactive floor plan with desk positions and colleague pins.
---

The floor plan view provides a visual way to find and book desks. Instead of scrolling through a list, you can see the office layout and click directly on a desk to book it.

> The floor plan view requires a **Pro** plan and must be set up by your admin.

## Using the floor plan

1. Navigate to **Desks** in the sidebar
2. Select a location and floor
3. Switch to the **Floor Plan** view (desktop only)
4. The office layout appears with desk markers showing availability:
   - **Available** — Desk is open for booking
   - **Booked** — Already reserved by someone
   - **Your booking** — A desk you've reserved
5. Click an available desk to open the booking dialog
6. Confirm your booking

## Colleague pins

When colleagues have booked desks on the same floor, their avatars appear as pins on the floor plan. This helps you find and book a desk near your team.

## Zone highlighting

Desks are grouped into zones (Quiet Zone, Collaboration, Focus, etc.). Zones are visually highlighted on the floor plan with colored backgrounds, making it easy to find the right type of workspace.

## Neighborhoods

Named desk neighborhoods (e.g., "Sales Team Corner", "Engineering Area") are displayed as labeled regions on the floor plan.

## Mobile

The floor plan view is optimized for desktop screens. On mobile devices, the list view is shown instead.
