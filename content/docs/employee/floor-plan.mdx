title: Floor Plan View
description: Book desks using an interactive floor plan — coming soon.
---

The floor plan view will provide a visual way to find and book desks. Instead of scrolling through a list, you'll be able to see the office layout and click directly on a desk to book it.

> The floor plan view is **coming soon** and not yet available.

## Planned features

- **Interactive office map** — See the layout of your office floor with desk positions
- **Click-to-book** — Click on an available desk to open the booking dialog
- **Colleague pins** — See where your colleagues are booked with avatar pins on the map
- **Zone highlighting** — Desks grouped by zone type (Quiet Zone, Collaboration, Focus, etc.) with color-coded backgrounds
- **Neighborhoods** — Named desk areas displayed as labeled regions

## Current alternative

In the meantime, use the **list view** on the Desks page to browse and book desks. The list view supports the same filters (location, floor, equipment, zone, availability) and provides all booking functionality.

The floor plan view will require a **Pro** plan.
