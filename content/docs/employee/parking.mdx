---
title: Parking
description: Book parking spots for your office days — car, bike, motorcycle, or EV charging.
---

Presentify's parking feature lets you reserve a parking spot alongside your desk booking. Parking requires a **Pro** plan and must be enabled by your workspace admin.

## Parking spot types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Car** | Standard car parking space |
| **Bike** | Bicycle parking |
| **Motorcycle** | Motorcycle parking |
| **EV Charging** | Parking space with electric vehicle charging |

Additional amenities on spots:
- **Accessible** — Wheelchair-accessible parking
- **EV Charging** — Electric vehicle charging capability

## Booking modes

Your admin configures one of two parking modes per location:

### Basic mode

In basic mode, parking works as an add-on to desk booking:

1. Book a desk as usual
2. Check the **Add Parking** checkbox in the booking dialog
3. A pool parking spot is automatically assigned to you

You don't choose a specific spot — the system assigns the next available one. Cancelling your desk booking also cancels the linked parking booking.

### Advanced mode

In advanced mode, you select a specific parking spot:

1. Navigate to **Parking** in the sidebar
2. Select a location and date
3. Browse available spots (filter by type, accessibility, EV charging)
4. Click a spot to open the booking dialog
5. Confirm your booking

## Filters

- **Spot Type** — Car, Bike, Motorcycle, EV Charging
- **Accessible** — Show only accessible spots
- **EV Charging** — Show only spots with charging

## My Bookings

Your parking bookings appear in the **My Bookings** tab alongside desk bookings. You can:

- **Check In** — Confirm your arrival (if check-in is required)
- **Cancel** — Cancel the booking
- **Share** — Share a direct link to your parking booking (available on confirmed and checked-in bookings). On mobile devices, the system share sheet opens so you can send the link via any app. On desktop, the link is copied to your clipboard automatically. The link opens the parking page pre-filtered to the same date and location, so your colleague can book a nearby spot in one click.
