The Presence page is where you manage your weekly work schedule. Set your status for each day, associate a location, and see what your colleagues have planned.

## Available statuses

Presentify offers seven presence statuses:

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **In Office** | Working from the office |
| **Remote** | Working from home or another remote location |
| **On the Road** | Traveling for work |
| **Customer Site** | Working at a customer's location |
| **Training / Event** | Attending a training session or event |
| **Onsite without Desk** | At the office but not at a desk (meetings, etc.) |
| **Away** | Out of office (vacation, sick, etc.) |

Only **In Office** and **Onsite without Desk** count toward your office day compliance target.

## Planner tab

### Setting your status

1. Navigate to **Presence** in the sidebar
2. In the **My Week** card, click a day
3. Select a status from the picker
4. Optionally choose a location
5. Your status is saved immediately

### Half-day support

You can set your status for the full day, morning only, or afternoon only. In the status picker, toggle between **Full Day**, **Morning**, and **Afternoon** before selecting a status.

Half-day entries show a badge indicating which part of the day (e.g., "Morning — In Office").

### Bulk-set multiple days

To set the same status for several days at once:

1. Hold **Shift** and click multiple days in the week grid
2. An action bar appears at the bottom showing "Selected X days"
3. Click **Bulk Set**
4. Choose a status and location
5. All selected days are updated at once

You can also use the **Bulk Set** button in the top-right of the My Week card.

### Location association

If your workspace has locations configured, you can associate a location with each presence entry. This helps colleagues know which office you're at. The location is shown on your dashboard, in the team calendar, and in Who's In.

### Office day compliance

If your admin has set a minimum office days requirement, a compliance badge appears on the My Week card:

- **Green** — You meet or exceed the requirement
- **Amber** — You're one day short
- **Red** — You're below the requirement

The badge shows "X/Y office days required" to help you plan.

## Colleagues Week

Above the planner, you can see your colleagues' presence for the selected week. Each row shows a colleague with color-coded status dots for each day.

You can filter colleagues by:

- **All Colleagues** — Everyone in the workspace
- **My Team** — Your team members
- **Favorites** — Starred colleagues

Click a colleague's row to expand it and see full status labels, locations, and booking details.

## History tab

The History tab shows a monthly calendar view of your past presence entries:

- Each day is color-coded by status
- A small dot indicates days with desk or parking bookings
- Four stats cards show your monthly totals: In Office, Remote, Away, and Check-In Rate

Use the month navigation arrows to browse past months.
