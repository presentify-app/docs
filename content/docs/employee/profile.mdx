---
title: Profile & Settings
description: Manage your profile picture, desk preferences, notification settings, and privacy.
---

Your profile page lets you personalize your Presentify experience. Access it by clicking your avatar in the sidebar and selecting **Profile**.

## Profile picture

You have three options:

- **Upload a custom picture** — JPG, PNG, or WebP, max 5 MB
- **Use your Microsoft photo** — If you signed in with Microsoft, you can sync your profile picture
- **Initials fallback** — If no picture is set, your initials are shown

## Language & timezone

- **Language** — Switch between English and German. Affects the UI language and email notifications.
- **Timezone** — Set your timezone for correct date/time display across bookings and presence.

## Theme

Choose between **Light**, **Dark**, or **System** (follows your device setting).

## Default location

Set a default office location. When you open the desk or parking booking page, this location is pre-selected. Useful if you always work from the same office.

## Desk preferences

Pre-configure your preferred desk equipment and zone types. When you open the desk booking page, these preferences are applied as default filters.

**Equipment preferences:**
Monitor, Docking Station, Dual Screen, Standing Desk, Ergonomic, Accessible, Quiet Zone, Window Seat

**Zone preferences:**
Neighborhood, Quiet Zone, Collaboration, Focus, Social, Meeting

Click a preference to toggle it on (filled) or off (outline).

## Notification preferences

Control which notifications you receive. Each type can be toggled on or off:

**Bookings:**
- Booking confirmed
- Booking cancelled (always on)
- Booking released (always on)
- No-show warning (always on)

**Check-in:**
- Booking checked in
- Check-in reminder

**Delegate & Guest:**
- Delegate booking created
- Delegate booking cancelled
- Guest booking created
- Guest booking reminder

**Presence:**
- Presence reminder
- Office day reminder
- Team office day notification

**Digest:**
- Weekly digest (Monday morning summary)

Some notification types are always enabled and cannot be turned off (marked as "always on").

## Privacy settings

Control your visibility to colleagues:

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Show in Who's In** | Allow others to see you in the Who's In view |
| **Show Desk on Map** | Allow others to see your desk location on floor plans |
| **Show in Colleagues Week** | Allow others to see you in the team calendar |
| **Default Booking Private** | New bookings are marked as private by default |

These settings are personal and override workspace-level privacy rules for your own data.
