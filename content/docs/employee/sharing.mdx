title: Sharing & Deep Links
description: Share your bookings with colleagues via link.
---

You can share your desk and parking bookings with colleagues so they can find you or book a nearby desk.

## Sharing a booking

1. Go to **Desks** > **My Bookings** (or the Dashboard)
2. Find the booking you want to share
3. Click the **Share** button (share icon)
4. The booking link is copied to your clipboard (or opens your device's share sheet on mobile)

The share text includes the resource name and date, e.g., "I've booked Desk A2 on March 20. Check it out!"

## What the link does

When a colleague opens the link, they're taken to the desk or parking booking page with:

- The correct **date** pre-selected
- The correct **location** pre-selected

This makes it easy for them to find a desk near you or see where you'll be working.

## Privacy

If your booking is marked as **private**, the share link still works but colleagues won't see your name on the booking in the general booking list. Only direct link recipients can see the context.

If you're not comfortable sharing your location, you can disable **Show Desk on Map** in your [Profile privacy settings](/docs/employee/profile).
