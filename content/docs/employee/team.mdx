---
title: Team Overview
description: View your team's weekly schedule, coverage stats, and find overlap days.
---

The Team page helps you coordinate with your team. See who's working where, compare schedules, and find the best days for in-person collaboration.

## Team calendar

The main view shows a weekly calendar with all team members. Each row represents a colleague, with color-coded status dots for each day (Monday through Sunday).

- Click a row to expand it and see full status labels, locations, and booking indicators
- Use the search bar to find a specific colleague
- Navigate between weeks using the arrow buttons

Desk bookings show a monitor icon and parking bookings show a car icon below the status dot.

## Coverage cards

Five cards (Monday through Friday) show daily coverage at a glance:

- **In Office** count with building icon
- **Remote** count with home icon
- **Not Planned** count for members who haven't set a status

Today's card is highlighted. The percentage indicates how many team members are in the office.

## Schedule compare

To compare your schedule with a colleague:

1. Find the colleague in the team calendar
2. Click the compare button (arrows icon) next to their name
3. A comparison panel opens showing your schedule and theirs side by side
4. Overlap days (both in office) are highlighted in green
5. A badge shows how many overlap days you share

This helps you plan meetings or find the best days to work together in person.

## Team overlap heatmap

A monthly calendar view showing daily office coverage as a color-coded heatmap:

- Darker colors indicate higher in-office attendance
- Hover over a day to see exact counts
- Navigate between months with arrow buttons

Use this to identify patterns in your team's office attendance over time.

## Team days

If your admin has set up team days (Pro plan), you'll see them on the calendar:

- Team days are highlighted with a violet border
- The **Upcoming Team Days** section lists future team days with attendance vs. target

Team days are recurring office days set by your manager or admin to encourage in-person collaboration on specific weekdays.

## Plan Next Week

If team members haven't planned their presence for next week yet, a banner appears with their names, reminding the team to set their status.
