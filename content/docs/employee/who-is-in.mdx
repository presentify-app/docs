title: Who's In
description: See who's in the office today, find colleagues, and check occupancy stats.
---

The Who's In page gives you a real-time view of who's planned to be in the office on any given day. Use it to find colleagues, plan collaborative work, or check office occupancy.

> Who's In may need to be enabled by your admin. If you don't see it in the sidebar, your workspace may have this feature disabled.

## Day navigation

Use the arrow buttons to switch between days, or click **Today** or **Tomorrow** for quick access.

## Occupancy stats

Four cards at the top show the day's numbers:

- **In Office** — Colleagues marked as In Office or Onsite without Desk
- **Remote** — Colleagues working remotely
- **Other** — Colleagues with other statuses (Away, On the Road, etc.)
- **Total Planned** — All colleagues who have set any status

## Colleague list

Each colleague card shows:

- **Name** and avatar
- **Status** badge with color-coded icon
- **Location** (if set)
- **Team** name
- **Half-day** badge (Morning or Afternoon, if applicable)
- **New to team** badge (for members who joined within the last 30 days)

### Book nearby

If a colleague has booked a desk, you'll see a **Book Near** button. Click it to jump to the desk booking page filtered to the same floor, making it easy to sit near your colleague.

### Favorites

Click the star icon to favorite a colleague. Favorites are sorted to the top of the list and can be filtered separately.

## Filters

- **Search** — Type a name or email to find a specific colleague
- **Team** — Filter by a specific team, or select "Favorites" to show only starred colleagues
- **Status** — Filter by presence status (In Office, Remote, etc.)
- **Location** — Filter by office location

## Location occupancy

Below the colleague list, a weekly occupancy chart shows how many people are expected at each location across the current week.
