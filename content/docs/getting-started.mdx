This guide walks you through setting up Presentify from your first sign-in to inviting your team. If you're a new administrator, start here.

## Step 1: Create your account

Go to [presentify.org](https://presentify.org) and sign up. You have two options:

- **Sign in with Microsoft** — Uses your Microsoft 365 account. Recommended if your organization uses Microsoft 365, as it enables calendar sync and Entra ID integration later.
- **Sign in by email** — Enter your email address and receive a sign-in code. No Microsoft account required.

After signing in, you'll be prompted to create your first workspace.

## Step 2: Create your workspace

Enter a workspace name (e.g., your company name). Presentify will suggest a name based on your email domain or Microsoft profile.

You'll also set:

- **Default language** — English or German. Affects emails and notifications sent to members.
- **Default timezone** — Used for all scheduling and booking displays.

> After creation, you'll be guided through the [Setup Wizard](/docs/setup) to configure your workspace step by step.

## Step 3: Add your office locations

In the Setup Wizard, add your office locations:

1. Enter a location name (e.g., "Berlin Office")
2. Optionally add the city
3. Add as many locations as you need

Each location can later have buildings, floors, and desks assigned to it.

## Step 4: Set up desks

For each location, you can auto-generate a desk layout:

1. Enter a building name (default: "Main Building")
2. Define floor names (one per line, e.g., "Ground Floor", "1st Floor")
3. Set how many desks per floor (default: 10, max 100)

Presentify will create the building, floors, and desks automatically. You can customize them in detail later under [Admin > Desks](/docs/admin/desks).

## Step 5: Invite your team

Add team members by email or share an invite link:

- **Email invites** — Enter email addresses one by one. Each person receives a sign-in link.
- **Invite link** — Copy a shareable link and send it via Slack, Teams, or email. Anyone with the link can join your workspace.

Invited members start with the **Member** role. You can [change roles](/docs/roles) at any time.

## Step 6: Configure privacy

Choose how much information is visible to colleagues:

- **Full** — Everyone sees everyone's name, status, desk, and location.
- **Restricted** — Team members see full details, others see limited info.
- **Minimal** — Only anonymous aggregates are shown.

You can also enable or disable **guest booking** and the **weekly digest email**.
