## Create Your Workspace

1. Go to [presentify.org](https://presentify.org) and sign up
2. Choose your authentication method (Microsoft or Magic Link)
3. Create your first workspace

## Invite Your Team

Once your workspace is set up, invite team members:

1. Navigate to **Settings** → **Team**
2. Click **Invite Members**
3. Enter email addresses or share the invite link

## Set Up Locations

Define your office locations to enable desk booking:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Locations**
2. Add your office locations with address and details
3. Optionally upload a floor plan
