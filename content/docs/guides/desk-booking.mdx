## Overview

Presentify's desk booking feature lets your team reserve workspaces in advance, helping coordinate hybrid work schedules.

## Booking a Desk

1. Navigate to **Desks** in the sidebar
2. Select a date
3. Choose an available desk from the floor plan or list view
4. Confirm your booking

## Floor Plans

Admins can upload floor plans to provide a visual booking experience:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Locations**
2. Select a location and click **Floor Plan**
3. Upload your floor plan image
4. Place desk markers on the plan

## Booking Rules

Workspace admins can configure booking rules:

- **Advance booking limit** — How far in advance desks can be booked
- **Check-in required** — Whether users must check in on arrival
- **Auto-release** — Automatically release unclaimed bookings
