## Overview

Presentify's parking feature lets employees reserve parking spots alongside their desk bookings.

## Booking a Parking Spot

1. Navigate to **Parking** in the sidebar
2. Select a date
3. Choose an available parking spot
4. Confirm your booking

## Managing Parking Spots

Admins can configure parking:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Locations**
2. Select a location and configure **Parking**
3. Define the number of available spots
4. Optionally upload a parking plan
