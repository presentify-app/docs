## Overview

Presence tracking helps teams see who's working where — office, remote, or out of office — making it easy to plan collaborative days.

## Setting Your Status

1. Navigate to **Presence** in the sidebar
2. Select a day in the current or upcoming week
3. Choose your status: **Office**, **Remote**, or **Off**
4. Your team can now see your plans

## Team Overview

The presence dashboard shows your team's week at a glance:

- **Color-coded statuses** for quick scanning
- **Filter by location** to see who's at a specific office
- **Department view** for larger organizations

## Who's In

The **Who's In** page provides a real-time view of who's in the office today, making it easy to find colleagues.
