## General Settings

Access workspace settings via **Settings** in the sidebar.

### Workspace Details

- **Name** — Your workspace display name
- **Timezone** — Default timezone for the workspace
- **Logo** — Upload your company logo

### Locations

Manage your office locations:

- Add, edit, or remove locations
- Configure floor plans and parking for each location
- Set location-specific booking rules

## Team Management

### Inviting Members

1. Go to **Settings** → **Team**
2. Click **Invite Members**
3. Enter email addresses or share the invite link

### Roles

- **Admin** — Full workspace management access
- **Member** — Can book desks, set presence, and view team

## Integrations

### Microsoft 365

Presentify integrates with Microsoft 365:

- **Entra ID Sync** — Automatically sync users from your directory
- **Calendar Sync** — Sync desk bookings to Outlook calendars
- **Teams** — Presence status in Microsoft Teams

### Webhooks

Set up outgoing webhooks to integrate Presentify events with your systems. See [API Documentation](https://presentify.org) for details.
