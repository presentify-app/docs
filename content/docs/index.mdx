Welcome to the Presentify documentation. Here you'll find everything you need to get started, manage your workspace, and make the most of your hybrid work setup.

## What is Presentify?

Presentify helps organizations coordinate hybrid work. Your team can plan office days, book desks and parking spots, and see who's in the office — all in one place.

**Core features:**

- **Presence Planning** — Set your weekly status (Office, Remote, Away) and see your team's plans at a glance.
- **Desk Booking** — Reserve desks with interactive floor plans, filters by equipment and zone, and QR code check-in.
- **Parking** — Book parking spots alongside your desk reservations.
- **Team Coordination** — See who's in the office, plan team days, and find colleagues.
- **Analytics** — Track office usage, identify trends, and optimize your workspace.
- **Microsoft 365 Integration** — Sync with Entra ID, Outlook calendars, and Microsoft Teams.
