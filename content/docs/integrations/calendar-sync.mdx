Presentify provides an iCalendar (ICS) feed that you can subscribe to in any calendar app. This gives you a read-only view of your presence and bookings alongside your regular calendar.

For step-by-step setup instructions, see [Calendar Subscription](/docs/employee/calendar-subscription) in the Employee Guide.

## How it works

Each member can generate a unique calendar token in their profile. This token creates a personal URL:

```
webcal://presentify.org/api/calendar/{token}
```

Your calendar app polls this URL periodically and displays your Presentify events.

## What's included

The calendar feed contains events for:

- **Presence entries** — Your daily status (In Office, Remote, Away, etc.)
- **Desk bookings** — With desk name and location details
- **Parking bookings** — With spot name and location

## Time range

The feed includes:

- **30 days in the past** — Recent history
- **90 days in the future** — Upcoming schedule

## Limitations

- **Read-only** — Changes must be made in Presentify. Editing or deleting events in your calendar app has no effect.
- **No write-back** — Presentify does not create events in your Outlook or Google calendar directly. It only provides a subscription feed.
- **Polling interval** — Calendar apps typically refresh subscriptions every 15–60 minutes. New bookings may take a few minutes to appear.
- **Rate limited** — 10 requests per 60 seconds per IP address to prevent abuse.

## Security

- Calendar tokens are unique per member and can be [revoked](/docs/employee/calendar-subscription) at any time
- Revoking a token immediately invalidates the old URL
- A new token can be generated after revoking
- Tokens do not expire automatically
