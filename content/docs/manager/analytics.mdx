title: Analytics

The Analytics dashboard gives you insights into how your workspace uses office space. Track attendance patterns, compare locations, and measure adoption.

> Analytics requires a **Pro** plan and the **Manager**, **Office Admin**, **Admin**, or **Owner** role.

## KPI cards

Four headline metrics at the top of the dashboard:

| KPI | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| **Total Attendance** | Total in-office days during the selected period. Compares to the previous period of equal length. |
| **In-Office Rate** | Average daily percentage of members in the office. |
| **Peak Day** | The date with the highest in-office attendance and the count. |
| **Active Users** | Distinct members who set at least one presence entry, shown as "X of Y total members." |

## Charts

### Attendance by Weekday

A horizontal bar chart showing average in-office and remote attendance per weekday (Monday through Friday). Helps identify which days are most popular for office work.

### Attendance Trend

A line chart tracking in-office and remote counts over time. Displays daily data points for ranges up to 30 days, and weekly aggregates for longer ranges.

### Location Breakdown

A horizontal bar chart showing average daily in-office attendance per location. This chart always shows all locations regardless of the location filter.

### Team Breakdown

A horizontal bar chart showing the in-office rate percentage per team. For privacy, only teams with **3 or more members** are included.

## Adoption metrics

Below the charts, four adoption stats are displayed:

- **Total Members** — Active workspace members
- **Weekly Active Users (WAU)** — Members with at least one entry in the last 7 days
- **Monthly Active Users (MAU)** — Members with at least one entry in the last 30 days
- **Avg. Entries per User** — Average number of presence entries per member in the period

## Filters

- **Date Range** — Presets: Last 7 days, Last 30 days, Last 90 days. Custom range with a maximum of 90 days.
- **Location** — Filter by a specific location or view all locations.

## CSV Export

Export data for further analysis. Three export types are available:

| Export | Columns |
|--------|---------|
| **Attendance Data** | Date, In Office, Remote, Total |
| **Location Data** | Location, Total In Office, Days with Data, Avg Daily In Office |
| **Team Data** | Team, Members, In Office Days, Total Days, In Office Rate % |

Files are named `{type}_{startDate}_{endDate}.csv` and include a BOM for Excel compatibility.
