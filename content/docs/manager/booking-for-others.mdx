---
title: Booking for Others
description: Reserve desks and parking spots on behalf of your team members or guests.
---

As a Manager, you can book desks and parking for your direct reports. Admins and Owners can book for any workspace member.

## Who can book for whom?

| Your Role | Can Book For |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Member** | Only yourself |
| **Manager** | Your direct reports (members with you set as their manager) |
| **Office Admin** | Members at your assigned locations |
| **Admin** | Any workspace member |
| **Owner** | Any workspace member |

## Booking a desk for a colleague

1. Navigate to **Desks** and find an available desk
2. Click the desk to open the booking dialog
3. Under **Book for**, select **Colleague**
4. Search for and select the team member
5. Optionally add notes or toggle privacy
6. Click **Confirm**

The booking is created in the colleague's name. They'll receive a notification about the booking.

## Booking a desk for a guest

Admins and Managers can book for external visitors:

1. Open the booking dialog for a desk
2. Under **Book for**, select **Guest**
3. Enter the guest's **name** (required)
4. Optionally enter their **email** and **company**
5. Click **Confirm**

Guest bookings count toward the daily guest booking limit (default: 3 per day per location).

## Delegate parking bookings

The same delegation rules apply to parking bookings. In locations with advanced parking mode, select a parking spot and choose "Colleague" in the booking dialog.

In basic parking mode, the parking add-on checkbox is available when booking a desk for a colleague.

## Cancelling delegated bookings

You can cancel bookings you've made for others from the **My Bookings** tab or from the **Location Bookings** view (Office Admin and above).
