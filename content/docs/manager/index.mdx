As a Manager in Presentify, you have everything a regular member has, plus additional tools to coordinate and support your team.

## What Managers can do

In addition to all [employee features](/docs/employee), Managers can:

- **View Analytics** — Access the analytics dashboard with attendance trends, location breakdown, and team stats (Pro plan)
- **Plan Team Days** — Create recurring office days for your team (Pro plan)
- **Book for direct reports** — Reserve desks and parking on behalf of team members you manage
- **View Location Bookings** — See all bookings at your locations (if also Office Admin)

## Who is a Manager?

The Manager role is assigned by your workspace Admin or Owner in **Settings** > **Members**. Managers can see analytics and book for their direct reports — members whose manager is set to you in the member settings.

Managers cannot access workspace settings, manage members, or configure policies. For those capabilities, see the [Admin Guide](/docs/admin).
