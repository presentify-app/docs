---
title: Location Bookings
description: View and manage all desk and parking bookings at your locations.
---

The Location Bookings view shows all bookings at a specific location. It's available to Office Admins (for their assigned locations), Admins, and Owners.

## Accessing location bookings

1. Navigate to **Desks** or **Parking** in the sidebar
2. Switch to the **Location Bookings** tab

## What you see

Bookings are grouped by date and show:

- **Resource name** — Desk or parking spot identifier
- **Resource type** — Desk (monitor icon) or Parking (car icon)
- **Member name** — Who made the booking
- **Status badge** — Confirmed, Checked In, Completed, Cancelled, No Show, or Auto Released
- **Guest badge** — If the booking is for an external guest

## Filters

- **Date range** — Start and end date for the booking view
- **Resource type** — Filter by desks only or parking only
- **Status** — Filter by booking status

## Cancelling bookings

For bookings with status **Confirmed**, a cancel button is available. When you cancel:

1. Click the cancel button on the booking
2. Confirm in the dialog
3. The booking is cancelled and the member is notified
4. The desk or parking spot becomes available again

You can only cancel confirmed bookings. Checked-in, completed, or already cancelled bookings cannot be modified.

## Access scope

- **Office Admin** — Sees bookings only at locations assigned to you. You cannot view bookings at other locations.
- **Admin / Owner** — Sees bookings at all workspace locations.

The view shows up to 500 bookings per request.
