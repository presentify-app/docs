The Team Calendar gives you a complete picture of your team's office presence. Use it to plan meetings, identify collaboration opportunities, and monitor attendance patterns.

For basic team calendar features available to all members, see the [Employee Team Overview](/docs/employee/team). This page covers additional context for managers.

## Coverage cards

Five cards (Monday through Friday) give you a daily breakdown:

- **In Office** — Number and percentage of team members planned in office
- **Remote** — Number working remotely
- **Not Planned** — Members without a status set

Color coding helps you quickly spot low-attendance days: green for high coverage, amber for moderate, and red for low.

## Schedule compare

As a manager, schedule compare is particularly useful for planning 1:1s and team meetings:

1. Click the compare button next to any team member
2. Your schedule appears alongside theirs
3. Overlap days (both in office) are highlighted in green
4. The overlap count helps you identify the best days for in-person meetings

## Overlap heatmap

The monthly heatmap shows office attendance patterns across your team over time. Darker colors indicate higher attendance. Use this to:

- Identify which weekdays consistently have the best in-person coverage
- Spot declining attendance trends early
- Plan team events on high-attendance days

## Plan Next Week banner

When team members haven't planned their presence for the upcoming week, a reminder banner appears showing who still needs to set their status. This helps you encourage your team to plan ahead.
