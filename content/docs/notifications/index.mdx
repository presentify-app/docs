Presentify sends notifications to keep you informed about bookings, check-ins, presence reminders, and team activity. You can customize which notifications you receive.

## Notification center

Click the **bell icon** in the top navigation to open the notification center. It shows:

- A list of your recent notifications (newest first, 20 per page)
- An **unread count** badge on the bell icon
- Click a notification to mark it as read

## Notification types

### Bookings

| Type | Description | Always on? |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| **Booking confirmed** | Your desk or parking booking was confirmed | No |
| **Booking cancelled** | Your booking was cancelled (by you or an admin) | Yes |
| **Booking released** | Your booking was auto-released due to no-show | Yes |
| **No-show warning** | You haven't checked in and may lose your booking | Yes |

### Check-in

| Type | Description | Always on? |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| **Booking checked in** | Confirmation that you checked in | No |
| **Check-in reminder** | Reminder to check in at your booked desk | No |

### Delegate & Guest

| Type | Description | Always on? |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| **Delegate booking created** | Someone booked a desk for you | No |
| **Delegate booking cancelled** | A booking made for you was cancelled | No |
| **Guest booking created** | A guest booking was created by you | No |
| **Guest booking reminder** | Reminder about an upcoming guest booking | No |

### Presence

| Type | Description | Always on? |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| **Presence reminder** | Reminder to set your presence for the week | No |
| **Office day reminder** | Reminder that today is a planned office day | No |
| **Team office day** | Notification when a team day is coming up | No |

### Digest

| Type | Description | Always on? |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| **Weekly digest** | Monday morning summary email | No |

## Managing preferences

Each notification type can be toggled on or off in your [Profile settings](/docs/employee/profile) under **Notification Preferences**.

Types marked "Always on" cannot be disabled — they are critical for booking integrity (e.g., cancellation notices, no-show warnings).

## Admin controls

Workspace admins can:

- Enable or disable the **weekly digest** workspace-wide in [General Settings](/docs/admin/general)
- Set notification defaults for new members in **Member Defaults**
- Override individual member notification preferences in **Settings** > **Members** > **Edit Settings**
