The weekly digest is an email sent every **Monday morning** summarizing your upcoming week in Presentify.

## What's included

The digest email contains:

- **Your week at a glance** — Presence status for each day (if set)
- **Your bookings** — Upcoming desk and parking bookings
- **Team activity** — How many team members are planned in the office this week
- **Team days** — Upcoming team days with attendance targets

## Who receives it

The weekly digest is sent to all active members who have it enabled. It's controlled at two levels:

1. **Workspace level** — Admins can enable or disable the digest for the entire workspace in [General Settings](/docs/admin/general)
2. **Member level** — Each member can opt out in their [Profile](/docs/employee/profile) under Notification Preferences

Both must be enabled for the member to receive the digest. If the workspace toggle is off, no one receives it regardless of personal settings.

## Timing

The digest is sent on **Monday mornings** (based on the workspace's default timezone). It covers the upcoming work week (Monday through Friday).

## Disabling

### As a member

1. Go to your **Profile**
2. Scroll to **Notification Preferences**
3. Toggle off **Weekly Digest**

### As an admin

1. Go to **Settings** > **General**
2. Toggle off **Weekly Digest**

This disables the digest for all members, regardless of their personal settings.
