## Free Starter

The Free Starter plan is designed for small teams getting started with hybrid work. It includes:

- Up to **25 members**, **1 location**, **3 floors**, and **25 desks**
- Presence planning (Office, Remote, Away)
- Desk pool booking (shared desks, first-come-first-served)
- Team calendar and Who's In
- Notification center and calendar subscription (iCal)
- Microsoft or Magic Link authentication

Free Starter does not include individual desk assignments, parking, floor plans, analytics, check-in, policies, or team days.

## Pro

The Pro plan unlocks all workspace management features. It is billed per active user per month, with monthly or yearly billing options.

Everything in Free Starter, plus:

- **Unlimited** members, locations, floors, and desks
- **Individual desks** with fixed assignments
- **Parking** spot booking (car, bike, motorcycle, EV charging)
- **Floor plans** with interactive desk placement
- **Check-in** with manual or QR code mode, auto-release on no-show
- **Analytics** dashboard with attendance trends, location breakdown, and CSV export
- **Booking policies** per location (advance booking window, max bookings per day, check-in requirements)
- **Team days** for recurring office days
- **Desk assignments** (permanent or temporary)
- **Zone rules** with access control (Open, Restricted, Assigned)
- **Custom amenities** for desks and parking spots
- **Custom branding**
- **[Microsoft Teams Presence Sync](/docs/integrations/microsoft-teams-presence)** — Automatically sync planned presence to Microsoft Teams status

### What counts as an active user?

Presentify uses **monthly active user (MAU) billing**. A member is counted as active in a month if they created a non-cancelled booking or set a presence entry. Inactive members are not billed.

## Enterprise

The Enterprise plan adds developer and integration features on top of Pro. Contact us for pricing.

Everything in Pro, plus:

- **API access** — REST API v1 with API key authentication (rate limit: 150 requests/minute)
- **Webhooks** — Up to 5 endpoints with HMAC-SHA256 signature verification. Events for presence, desk bookings, parking bookings, and member changes.
- **SCIM provisioning** — Automated user provisioning from your identity provider

## 30-day Pro trial

Every new workspace automatically receives a 30-day Pro trial. During the trial, all Pro features are available at no cost.

After the trial ends:

- If you subscribe to Pro → your workspace continues with Pro features.
- If you don't subscribe → your workspace falls back to the Free Starter plan. No data is deleted, but features above the Free Starter limits become unavailable.
- You can upgrade to Pro at any time after the trial.

### Downgrade restrictions

Before downgrading from Pro to Free Starter, your workspace must meet the Free Starter limits:

- Maximum 25 members
- Maximum 1 location
- Maximum 3 floors
- Maximum 25 desks

If your workspace exceeds these limits, you'll need to reduce resources before downgrading.

## Billing

Presentify uses [Paddle](https://www.paddle.com) for payment processing. Paddle handles invoicing, tax calculation, and receipts.

You can manage your subscription under **Settings** > **Billing**.
