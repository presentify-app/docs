---
title: Roles & Permissions
description: Understand the five roles in Presentify and what each one can do.
---

Every workspace member has one of five roles. Roles determine what a member can see and do within the workspace.

## Role overview

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full control over the workspace. Only one Owner per workspace. |
| **Admin** | Same access as Owner, except cannot change the Owner role. |
| **Office Admin** | Manages bookings and QR codes for assigned locations only. |
| **Manager** | Can view analytics and book desks on behalf of direct reports. |
| **Member** | Standard user. Can plan presence, book desks, and manage their own profile. |

## Permission matrix

| Action | Member | Manager | Office Admin | Admin | Owner |
|--------|--------|---------|--------------|-------|-------|
| Plan own presence | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Book a desk for yourself | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Book for others | No | Direct reports | Members at assigned locations | Everyone | Everyone |
| View analytics (Pro) | No | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Manage location bookings | No | No | Assigned locations | All | All |
| Generate QR codes | No | No | Assigned locations | All | All |
| Manage workspace settings | No | No | No | Yes | Yes |
| Invite & remove members | No | No | No | Yes | Yes |
| Change member roles | No | No | No | Yes (not Owner) | Yes |
| Manage locations & desks | No | No | No | Yes | Yes |
| Configure policies | No | No | No | Yes | Yes |
| Manage billing | No | No | No | Yes | Yes |
| Transfer ownership | No | No | No | No | Yes |

## Role details

### Owner

Each workspace has exactly one Owner. The Owner has full access to everything, including the ability to transfer ownership to another member, delete the workspace, and manage billing.

The Owner role cannot be changed by Admins — only the current Owner can transfer ownership.

### Admin

Admins have the same access as the Owner, with one exception: they cannot change or remove the Owner. Admins can manage all workspace settings, invite members, configure locations, desks, parking, policies, and billing.

Use this role for IT administrators or HR leads who need full configuration access.

### Office Admin

Office Admins are scoped to specific locations. An Admin assigns one or more locations to an Office Admin. After that, the Office Admin can:

- View and cancel bookings at their assigned locations
- Generate and print QR codes for desks and parking at their locations

Office Admins **cannot** access workspace settings, manage members, or configure policies. They have no access to locations they are not assigned to.

### Manager

Managers can view analytics (requires Pro plan) and book desks or parking on behalf of their direct reports. A member's manager is configured in the member settings.

Managers cannot access workspace settings or manage locations. They see the same sidebar as regular members but have access to the analytics dashboard and delegation features.

### Member

Members are standard users. They can:

- Plan their own presence (Office, Remote, Away)
- Book desks and parking for themselves
- View team calendars and Who's In
- Manage their own profile, preferences, and notification settings
- Subscribe to calendar feeds (iCal)

Members cannot book on behalf of others or access admin features.

## Changing roles

Admins and Owners can change a member's role at any time:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Members**
2. Click the action menu next to a member
3. Select the new role

When changing a member to Office Admin, you'll be prompted to assign locations.

## Not sure which role you have?

Your role is shown in the workspace selector and on your profile page. If you need elevated access, ask your workspace Admin or Owner.
