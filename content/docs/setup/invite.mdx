Invite your team to join the workspace. You can add members one by one via email or share a link for self-service joining.

## Invite by email

1. Enter an **email address**
2. Click **Add**
3. The person receives a sign-in link via email
4. Repeat for additional members

Invited members appear in the list with an "Invited" badge. They become "Active" after their first sign-in.

## Invite link

The wizard shows a **shareable invite link** that you can copy and distribute:

1. Click **Copy Link** to copy the invite URL to your clipboard
2. Share it via Slack, Teams, email, or any messaging tool
3. Anyone who clicks the link can join your workspace after signing in

The invite link remains active and can be used by multiple people.

## Plan limits

- **Free Starter**: Up to 25 members total
- **Pro / Enterprise**: Unlimited members

If you've reached your plan's member limit, you'll need to [upgrade](/docs/admin/billing) before inviting more.

## Default role

All invited members start with the **Member** role. You can [change roles](/docs/admin/roles) after they join.

## Tips

- You don't need to invite everyone during setup — you can add members at any time via **Settings** > **Members**
- Invited members who haven't signed in yet can be removed and re-invited
- The invite link is the fastest way to onboard a large team
