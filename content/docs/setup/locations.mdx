Add your office locations so members know where to book desks and set their presence.

## Adding a location

1. Enter a **location name** (e.g., "Berlin Office", "Munich HQ")
2. Optionally enter the **city**
3. Click **Add** (or press Enter)
4. Repeat for additional locations

Locations appear in a list as you add them. Click the delete button to remove a location.

## How many locations?

- **Free Starter** plan: 1 location maximum
- **Pro / Enterprise**: Unlimited locations

Add at least one location to enable desk booking. You can skip this step and add locations later in [Settings > Locations](/docs/admin/locations).

## What about buildings and floors?

Buildings and floors are configured in the next step ([Desk Setup](/docs/setup/desks)) or later in Settings. This step only creates the top-level locations.

## Tips

- Use clear, recognizable names that your team will understand
- You can add detailed addresses, opening hours, and parking configuration later in Settings
- The first location in the list becomes the default selection for members
