The final configuration step sets your workspace's privacy defaults. These settings control how much information members can see about each other.

## Settings

### Privacy Level

Choose how much presence and booking information is visible to colleagues:

| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Full** | Everyone sees everyone's name, status, desk, and location |
| **Restricted** | Team members see full details, others see limited info |
| **Minimal** | Only anonymous aggregates, no individual data visible |

### Guest Booking

Toggle whether members can book desks for external guests. Default: **On**.

### Weekly Digest

Toggle the Monday morning summary email for all members. Default: **On**. Individual members can opt out in their profile settings.

## What happens next

Click **Save & Continue** to complete the Setup Wizard. You'll see a success screen showing:

- Number of locations added
- Number of teams created
- Number of members invited

From there, go to the **Dashboard** to start using Presentify, or to **Settings** to fine-tune your configuration.

## Changing later

All privacy settings can be adjusted at any time in [Settings > Privacy](/docs/admin/privacy). Additional options available in Settings include:

- Booking privacy (allow private bookings, default to private)
- Future booking visibility
- Data retention period (GDPR) and product analytics retention
- In-app satisfaction survey settings
- Feature toggles (parking, Who's In)
