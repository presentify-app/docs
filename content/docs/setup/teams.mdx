---

Create teams to group your members by department, function, or project. Teams are used for filtering in the team calendar, Who's In, and zone access rules.

## Adding a team

1. Enter a **team name** (e.g., "Engineering", "Sales", "Design")
2. Click **Add** (or press Enter)
3. Repeat for additional teams

Teams appear in a list as you add them. Click the delete button to remove a team.

## What teams are used for

- **Team Calendar** — Members can filter the team calendar by team
- **Who's In** — Filter colleagues by team
- **Zone Rules** — Restrict desk zones to specific teams
- **Analytics** — Team breakdown in the analytics dashboard
- **Coverage Cards** — Show in-office coverage per team

## Assigning members to teams

The wizard only creates teams — assigning members happens later:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Members**
2. Click the actions menu on a member
3. Select **Edit Settings**
4. Choose a team from the dropdown

Or set a **default team** in member defaults so new invites are auto-assigned.

## Tips

- You can skip this step and create teams later in Settings
- Teams can be renamed or deleted at any time
- Members can belong to one team at a time
