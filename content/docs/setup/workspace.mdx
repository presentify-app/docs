This is the first configuration step of the Setup Wizard. Set the fundamental settings that apply to your entire workspace.

## Fields

| Field | Default | Description |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| **Workspace Name** | Pre-filled from signup | Your company or team name (max 100 characters) |
| **Default Language** | English | Language for emails and notifications. English or German. |
| **Default Timezone** | Europe/Berlin | Timezone for scheduling and booking displays. Any IANA timezone. |
| **Allowed Auth Methods** | All | Which sign-in methods are available: All, Microsoft only, or Magic Link only |

## Tips

- The **workspace name** appears in the sidebar and workspace selector. Choose something recognizable to your team.
- The **default language** affects emails, notifications, and the default language for new members. Existing members can change their language in their profile.
- The **default timezone** is used as the default for new members. It also affects how dates and times are displayed across the workspace.
- If your organization requires corporate credentials, set **auth methods** to "Microsoft" to enforce Microsoft 365 sign-in.

## What happens next

Click **Save & Continue** to apply these settings and proceed to [Locations](/docs/setup/locations).

These settings can be changed later in [Settings > General](/docs/admin/general).
